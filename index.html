About Verified Badges

The verified badge is a check that appears next to an account's name to indicate that the account is authentic and has been verified by Meta. This helps people find the real accounts of public figures, celebrities, global brands, and other entities they might want to follow.

After your submission, we will review your verification request and contact you within 24-48 hours. The verification process helps us maintain a secure environment for all users and ensure that verified accounts represent authentic, notable entities.
